Design proposal · UPI Assistant 2.0 · rev B

The ledger on top.
The pay dock under your thumb.

A ground-up visual redesign that keeps everything the app already does — one-dial USSD payments, *99# verification, recipient memory — and grows it into three surfaces: Pay, Portfolio, and Money. Color direction: emerald on porcelain — the money-green lineage of Wise, Cash App, and Fi — in matched light and dark modes.

Grounding

Borrowed from accoladed flows, adapted for USSD

Five patterns from award-recognized finance apps, each translated to this app's offline-first reality.

Apple Card / WalletLedger-first home; transactions as the hero surface, grouped by day.
We take: feed on top, calm rows, big tabular numerals.
Google Pay (Tez)People-first recents row; repeat payments are one tap.
We take: recents avatars in the dock — known payees fire the one-shot dial.
Cash AppAmount entry as the entire screen; oversized keypad, money-green identity.
We take: the composer sheet — and the confidence to let green be the brand.
Robinhood (ADA winner)Scrubbable performance line, range pills, color tied to direction.
We take: the portfolio chart grammar — 1D→1Y pills, endpoint dot, P&L color.
Copilot Money (ADA finalist)Effortless categorization; a review inbox instead of forms.
We take: category chips, the "to review" queue, bill attachments.
Architecture

Four tabs, one dock

Everything that exists today lands intact in the Pay tab. Portfolio and Money ship as designed shells with placeholders until their backends arrive.

Pay · dark
1
Feed is the app. Newest first, day-grouped. Every row carries the verification verdict as a chip — the trust story this app uniquely earns via *99*6*1# is worn on the surface, not buried in receipts.
2
Balance is ambient, hidden by default. A masked pill in the top bar (existing balance-check flow feeds it). Tap the eye to reveal; long-press to refresh via *99*3#.
3
Recents row = the speed feature. Saved recipients with captured names. One tap opens the composer pre-filled — and because they're "known," payment fires the one-shot dial (~8s to PIN).
4
Two entries, one hierarchy. Scan QR is the emerald primary (merchant world); Number/UPI ID is the quiet secondary (people world). Both sit in the natural thumb arc — no reaching for a top corner, ever.
5
The dock is a living sheet. Pull the handle → composer. During payment the same sheet becomes the live status surface, so "where do I look" never changes.
6
Modes are siblings, not inverts. Light: deep emerald #0B6B4F on porcelain. Dark: luminous mint #34D88F on green-black. Chips, avatars and semantic colors are re-tuned per mode for contrast, not flipped.
Screen 2 · Composer

Amount entry that lives in the thumb zone

One sheet, everything reachable

Cash-App-scale numerals; the keypad and Pay never leave the bottom 60% of the screen. Shown in light mode.
1
Known payee = trust header. Captured name + number + "Known · pays in one dial" badge. Unknown payees show "Name will be verified by your bank during payment" instead — honest about the capture step.
2
Whole-rupee nudge. A subtle hint under the amount ("whole rupees pay fastest") because decimals force the slower interactive dial — the UI teaches the system's physics.
3
Remarks as a chip, not a form field. Optional, collapsed by default; digits-only remarks keep the one-shot path.
4
Pay is one thumb press. PIN is remembered for the session (personal device), so tapping Pay is genuinely the last touch until the USSD PIN prompt auto-fills.

Processing · dark

The current flow, given ceremony

This is the screen we already engineered — redesigned so the guarantees read at a glance.
1
Recipient name is the title (already shipped). Amount sits opposite — the two facts you'd check mid-payment.
2
Five honest steps, not USSD menus. The timeline names outcomes ("Bank responded — held as provisional"), not carrier dialogs. The active step pulses mint; done steps settle green.
3
The verification card is the brand moment. Every accoladed finance app sells trust; ours is literal — the network's own history is quoted before "success" is ever rendered.
4
Escape hatch. "Done — verify in background": the check continues, the ledger row shows the pulsing "checking" chip, and the row flips to ✓ verified when confirmed.
Screen 4 · Portfolio (Phase 2)

Your Yahoo Finance portfolio, native

Robinhood grammar, Indian market content

Backend imports the Yahoo portfolio; the app renders value, movement, and holdings. Shown in dark mode.
1
Hero = net value + day delta. One number, tabular numerals, delta chip in P&L color. Total return sits beneath in muted ink.
2
Scrubbable line, range pills. 1D · 1W · 1M · 3M · 1Y · All. Touch-drag shows value + date at the finger; the endpoint dot and delta re-color with direction. Chart is single-series — no legend, no grid noise.
3
Holdings rows carry three facts: position (qty × LTP), a 30-day sparkline, and day/total P&L. Tap → holding detail with the same chart grammar per symbol.
4
Offline honesty. Quotes carry a "as of 15:30 IST" stamp; stale data grays the delta rather than lying with old color.

Money · light · statement feed

The accounting surface

Bank-statement history from the backend, merged with — and checked against — the app's own UPI ledger.
1
Three-number header. In, Out, and Reconciled% for the month. Reconciled is this app's signature stat: statement rows auto-matched to in-app UPI records by RRN — the same matcher already built for SMS.
2
Review inbox, not forms. Copilot's best trick: uncategorized items queue in one place; each takes two taps (category chip → done). Rules learn: "IOCL → Fuel" applies forward automatically.
3
Every row can carry paper. 📎 attaches an invoice/bill (camera or PDF) stored against the transaction; the receipt page shows it beside the RRN.
4
Backend contract (thin). GET /statements?acct&month → normalized rows (date, narration, amount, ref). Parsing PDFs/CSVs happens server-side; the app stays offline-tolerant with cached months.
System

One status language everywhere

The same chips appear in the feed, on receipts, in Money, and on portfolio sync — state is never color-alone; every chip pairs an icon + word. Reversed is amber (a refund is a warm correction, not a violet mystery).

✓ Verifiedconfirmed via *99# history or bank SMS
◌ Checkingprovisional — verification in flight
↩ Reverseddebited, then refunded — both refs kept
✕ Failednetwork-confirmed failure — retry offered
Foundations

Tokens — one system, two modes

Each token is a light/dark pair (left = light, right = dark). Emerald is reserved for actions and brand moments; semantic money colors never appear on buttons. Dark mode is re-tuned, never auto-inverted.

Color — app

Ground #F2F5F2 / #0A0F0D
Surface #FFFFFF / #121A16
Brand emerald / mint #0B6B4F / #34D88F
Money-good #0B8A5C / #34D88F
Money-bad #C1443A / #F26A5B
Reversed #AE6A12 / #F0A44B
In-progress #75837C / #7C8D85

Green is deliberately both brand and money-good (the Cash App/Wise lineage): the brand tone is deep and sits on buttons; the semantic tone lives only in chips and deltas, always with icon + word.

Type

Display · 800numerals, titles
Body · 400–650rows, notes
Mono · RRN 062803971607refs

Recommend Bricolage Grotesque for display numerals (₹ amounts, hero values) with tabular figures everywhere digits align, system Roboto for body, and a mono face for RRNs and account masks. Scale: 44 / 32 / 20 / 16 / 13.5 / 11.

Shape · Motion · Modes

Sheet radius26dp
Card radius14–16dp
Chipsfull round
Dock springstiff, no bounce

Follows the system light/dark setting with an in-app override (Material 3 dynamic color off — the emerald identity is the brand). One signature motion: the dock's container-transform from strip → composer → live status. Everything honors reduced-motion.

Delivery

Three phases, nothing breaks

Phase 1 is a pure re-skin plus navigation shell — every existing flow (one-shot dial, verification, SMS reconcile, recipient memory) carries over untouched underneath.

Phase 1 · UI revamp — ships first

Pay tab + shell

  • Bottom nav with Portfolio/Money as designed placeholders ("Coming soon" states using the real layouts)
  • Home = ledger feed + pay dock (replaces current home)
  • Composer sheet replaces ConfirmationActivity's form
  • Processing timeline re-skins UssdInstructionActivity
  • Light + dark themes from the token pairs above
Phase 2 · Portfolio

Backend + charts

  • Import Yahoo Finance portfolio (backend job) · holdings API
  • Quote cache with as-of stamps for offline reads
  • Scrub chart 1D–All ranges, holding detail pages
Phase 3 · Money

Statements + accounting

  • Statement service (PDF/CSV → normalized rows)
  • RRN auto-reconcile against in-app UPI ledger
  • Categories + rules + review inbox
  • Attachments — invoices/bills on any transaction
